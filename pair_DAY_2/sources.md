## Tool or Engineering Pattern Used

OpenRouter API (2026) *meta-llama/llama-3.1-8b-instruct via OpenRouter gateway*. Available at: https://openrouter.ai (Accessed: 6 May 2026).

Five-trial controlled comparison of prompt-instructed JSON (Approach A) versus native tool_use (Approach B) on an AI maturity scoring task derived from the Week 10 Conversion Engine. Approach A produced valid output in all five runs (parser-rescued from markdown fencing); Approach B failed in all five runs due to missing tool-call support on the model/gateway combination.
